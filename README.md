# 📊 Business Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability.
The goal is to uncover patterns in sales, profit, and customer behavior to support better business decisions.

---

## 🧰 Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib / Seaborn
* Google Colab

---

## 📂 Dataset

Sample Superstore dataset (retail sales data including sales, profit, discount, and customer segments).

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Areas

* Sales performance by category and region
* Profitability analysis
* Impact of discounts on profit
* Customer segment behavior

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Certain product categories generate high sales but low or negative profit
* High discounts are often associated with reduced profitability
* Some regions outperform others in both sales and profit

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Reduce excessive discounting in low-margin categories
* Focus on high-performing regions and products
* Reassess products that consistently generate losses
